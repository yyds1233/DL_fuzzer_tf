tf.tensordot
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/tensordot
Signature: tf.tensordot(a, b, axes, name=None)

Tensor contraction over specified axes.

Shape
Input:
- a: shape (a0, a1, ..., ap)
- b: shape (b0, b1, ..., bq)

Output:
- if contracting axes A from a and B from b, output shape is the non-contracted dimensions of a followed by the non-contracted dimensions of b
- examples:
  - a (M, K), b (K, N), axes=1 -> (M, N)
  - a (B, M, K), b (B, K, N), axes=([2], [1]) -> (B, M, B, N) unless batch axes are handled separately

Notes:
- output rank depends on how many axes are contracted
