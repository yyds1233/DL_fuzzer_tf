tf.nn.separable_conv2d
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/nn/separable_conv2d
Signature: tf.nn.separable_conv2d(input, depthwise_filter, pointwise_filter, strides, padding, rate=None, name=None, data_format=None, dilations=None)

Computes a 2-D separable convolution: depthwise convolution followed by pointwise convolution.

Shape
Input:
- input: shape (N, H, W, C_in)
- depthwise_filter: shape (K_h, K_w, C_in, channel_multiplier)
- pointwise_filter: shape (1, 1, C_in * channel_multiplier, C_out)

Output:
- shape (N, H_out, W_out, C_out)

Constraints:
- depthwise and pointwise channel dimensions must line up exactly
