tf.math.unsorted_segment_sum
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/math/unsorted_segment_sum
Signature: tf.math.unsorted_segment_sum(data, segment_ids, num_segments, name=None)

Computes the sum along segments without requiring sorted segment ids.

Shape
Input:
- data: shape (N, d1, d2, ..., dk)
- segment_ids: shape (N,)
- num_segments: scalar integer

Output:
- shape (num_segments, d1, d2, ..., dk)

Constraints:
- segment_ids length must equal data.shape[0]
