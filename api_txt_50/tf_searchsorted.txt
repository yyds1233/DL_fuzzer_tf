tf.searchsorted
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/searchsorted
Signature: tf.searchsorted(sorted_sequence, values, side='left', out_type=tf.int32, name=None)

Finds insertion indices in a sorted sequence.

Shape
Input:
- sorted_sequence: shape (..., S)
- values: shape (..., V)

Output:
- indices with the same shape as values: (..., V)

Constraints:
- batch prefix before the last dimension should be broadcast-compatible or equal
- the last dimension of sorted_sequence is treated as the sorted axis
