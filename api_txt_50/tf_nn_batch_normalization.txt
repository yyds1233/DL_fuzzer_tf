tf.nn.batch_normalization
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/nn/batch_normalization
Signature: tf.nn.batch_normalization(x, mean, variance, offset, scale, variance_epsilon, name=None)

Normalizes x using mean and variance, then applies optional offset and scale.

Shape
Input:
- x: shape (N, C), (N, H, W, C), (N, C, H, W), or more generally (..., C) depending on layout
- mean: shape (C,) or broadcast-compatible shape
- variance: shape (C,) or broadcast-compatible shape
- offset: shape (C,) or broadcast-compatible shape
- scale: shape (C,) or broadcast-compatible shape

Output:
- same shape as x

Constraints:
- mean, variance, offset, and scale must be broadcast-compatible with x
