tf.transpose
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/transpose
Signature: tf.transpose(a, perm=None, conjugate=False, name='transpose')

Permutes the dimensions of a tensor.

Shape
Input:
- a: shape (d0, d1, ..., d(n-1))
- perm: shape (n), a permutation of (0, 1, ..., n-1)

Output:
- shape (d(perm[0]), d(perm[1]), ..., d(perm[n-1]))
- examples: (N, H, W, C) -> (N, C, H, W), (M, K) -> (K, M)

Constraints:
- rank of output equals rank of input
- perm length must equal rank(a)
