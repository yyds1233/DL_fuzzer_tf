tf.math.reduce_logsumexp
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/math/reduce_logsumexp
Signature: tf.math.reduce_logsumexp(input_tensor, axis=None, keepdims=False, name=None)

Computes log(sum(exp(input_tensor))) across dimensions.

Shape
Input:
- input_tensor: shape (d0, d1, ..., d(n-1))

Output:
- same rank rules as reduce_sum and reduce_mean
