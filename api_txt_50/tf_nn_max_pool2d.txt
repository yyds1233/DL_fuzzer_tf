tf.nn.max_pool2d
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/nn/max_pool2d
Signature: tf.nn.max_pool2d(input, ksize, strides, padding, data_format='NHWC', name=None)

Applies 2-D max pooling.

Shape
Input:
- input: shape (N, H, W, C) for NHWC or (N, C, H, W) for NCHW

Output:
- shape (N, H_out, W_out, C) for NHWC
- shape (N, C, H_out, W_out) for NCHW

Constraints:
- H_out and W_out depend on ksize, strides, and padding
- channel dimension C is preserved
