tf.math.segment_sum
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/math/segment_sum
Signature: tf.math.segment_sum(data, segment_ids, name=None)

Computes the sum along segments.

Shape
Input:
- data: shape (N, d1, d2, ..., dk)
- segment_ids: shape (N,)

Output:
- shape (num_segments, d1, d2, ..., dk)

Constraints:
- segment_ids length must equal data.shape[0]
- segment_ids are typically sorted and define contiguous segments
