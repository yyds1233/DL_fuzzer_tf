tf.meshgrid
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/meshgrid
Signature: tf.meshgrid(*args, indexing='xy', name=None)

Broadcasts 1-D coordinate vectors to coordinate grids.

Shape
Input:
- x: shape (Nx,)
- y: shape (Ny,)
- z: shape (Nz,)

Output:
- with two vectors, outputs two tensors of shape (Ny, Nx) for indexing='xy' or (Nx, Ny) for indexing='ij'
- with three vectors, outputs three tensors of shape (Nx, Ny, Nz) for indexing='ij'

Notes:
- output rank equals number of input coordinate vectors
