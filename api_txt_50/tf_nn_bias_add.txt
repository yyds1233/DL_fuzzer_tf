tf.nn.bias_add
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/nn/bias_add
Signature: tf.nn.bias_add(value, bias, data_format=None, name=None)

Adds a 1-D bias to a tensor.

Shape
Input:
- value: commonly shape (N, H, W, C) or (N, C, H, W), also supports N...C and NC...
- bias: shape (C,)

Output:
- same shape as value

Constraints:
- bias length must equal the channel dimension of value
