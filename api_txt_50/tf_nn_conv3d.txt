tf.nn.conv3d
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/nn/conv3d
Signature: tf.nn.conv3d(input, filters, strides, padding, data_format='NDHWC', dilations=None, name=None)

Computes a 3-D convolution.

Shape
Input:
- input: shape (N, D, H, W, C_in) for NDHWC or (N, C_in, D, H, W) for NCDHW
- filters: shape (K_d, K_h, K_w, C_in, C_out)

Output:
- shape (N, D_out, H_out, W_out, C_out) for NDHWC or (N, C_out, D_out, H_out, W_out) for NCDHW

Constraints:
- filter input channels C_in must match input channels
