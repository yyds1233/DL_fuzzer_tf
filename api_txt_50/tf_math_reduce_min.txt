tf.math.reduce_min
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/math/reduce_min
Signature: tf.math.reduce_min(input_tensor, axis=None, keepdims=False, name=None)

Reduces a tensor by taking the minimum across dimensions.

Shape
Input:
- input_tensor: shape (d0, d1, ..., d(n-1))

Output:
- same rank rules as reduce_sum and reduce_mean
