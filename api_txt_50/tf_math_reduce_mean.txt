tf.math.reduce_mean
Official URL: https://www.tensorflow.org/api_docs/python/tf/math/reduce_mean
Signature: tf.math.reduce_mean(input_tensor, axis=None, keepdims=False, name=None)

Reduces a tensor by averaging elements across dimensions.

Shape
Input:
- input_tensor: shape (d0, d1, ..., d(n-1))

Output:
- same rank rules as reduce_sum
- examples:
  - (N, C, H, W), axis=1 -> (N, H, W)
  - (N, T, D), axis=2, keepdims=True -> (N, T, 1)
